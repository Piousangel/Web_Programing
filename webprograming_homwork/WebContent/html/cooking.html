Cooking Soldier

Sgt. Whole Sgt.

I joined the military as a cook in the Capital Corps in 2016.
Before joining the army, I was a man who had never even caught a knife.

However, as I made 300 servings of food every day for 2 years after joining the army,

I naturally improved my cutting skills and cooking skills.
I am very proud to have served in the army as a cook, and I have been interested in cooking since then,
so I have been cooking as my hobby! I like meat best.
