I  love  playing Soccer & SON

member of national team

Tottenham team

Tottenham team

I have liked soccer since I was in elementary school. Every break, I went to the playground with a soccer ball, and my favorite lunch time was playing soccer for a long time.

On weekends, my mom, dad, older brother, and I kicked the ball together in the playground.

When I was in middle school and high school, we played soccer together, and when I came to college, I became a fan of Son. And then rooting for Son until now.

I went to the club and kicked the ball. Even in the military, whenever I had time, I gathered my senior successors and played soccer together. I can't leave soccer out of my life.
